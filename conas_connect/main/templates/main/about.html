About Conas Connect

Learn about our journey, mission, and the impact we're making in biotechnology education and research.

Our Story

Conas Connect was founded in 2022 by a group of passionate biotechnology students who recognized the need for a platform that connects students with opportunities in the field. What started as a small initiative at Makerere University has grown into a nationwide network of students, researchers, and industry professionals.

Our name "Conas" is derived from "Connection" and "Association," reflecting our core mission of connecting students with opportunities and fostering a community of like-minded individuals passionate about biotechnology.

Our Vision

To be a leading student-driven platform fostering innovation, research, and application of biotechnology for sustainable development in Uganda and beyond.

Our Mission

To empower students with knowledge, skills, and opportunities in biotechnology through education, research, networking, and community engagement, while promoting scientific excellence and ethical practices.

Our Core Values

What We Do

Education and Training

We organize workshops, seminars, and training sessions to equip students with practical skills in biotechnology. Our programs cover a wide range of topics, from basic laboratory techniques to advanced research methodologies.

  • Hands-on laboratory workshops
  • Guest lectures by industry experts
  • Online courses and webinars

Research Support

We facilitate research collaborations between students, faculty, and industry partners. Our platform connects students with research opportunities and provides resources to support their projects.

  • Research mentorship programs
  • Access to laboratory facilities
  • Research funding opportunities

Career Development

We connect students with internships, job opportunities, and career guidance in the biotechnology sector. Our network includes academic institutions, research organizations, and industry partners.

  • Internship placements
  • Job fairs and networking events
  • Resume building and interview preparation
