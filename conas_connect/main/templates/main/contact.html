Frequently Asked Questions

To become a member, you can register on our website or visit our office at Makerere University. Membership is open to all students, researchers, and professionals in biotechnology and related fields. There is a small annual membership fee that helps support our programs and activities.

We welcome partnerships with academic institutions, research organizations, industry partners, and NGOs. Please email us at partnerships@conasconnect.org with details about your organization and potential collaboration areas. Our partnerships team will get back to you to discuss opportunities.

We're always looking for volunteers to help with our events, outreach programs, and administrative tasks. Please fill out the volunteer form on our website or email us at volunteer@conasconnect.org with your interests and availability.

We strive to respond to all inquiries within 24-48 hours during business days. For urgent matters, please call our office directly at +256 700 123 456.

While we welcome visitors, we recommend scheduling an appointment to ensure that the appropriate team member is available to assist you. You can schedule an appointment by calling our office or sending an email to info@conasconnect.org.
